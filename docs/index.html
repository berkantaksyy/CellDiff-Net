ML-Based Identification of Stem Cell Differentiation States from Microscopy Images

Berkant Aksoy · Elif Burak · Sarya Rojin Gönüldaş
GitHub Slides PDF References Paper — Coming Soon

Abstract

Identifying stem cell differentiation states is a fundamental challenge in cell biology and regenerative medicine. This work presents a systematic review of ten recent publications spanning deep learning, classical machine learning, and hybrid approaches for analyzing stem cell morphology from microscopy images. We survey methods across brightfield, phase-contrast, confocal, and label-free imaging modalities, covering key tasks including cell segmentation, differentiation state classification, virtual staining, and quality control for clinical-grade iPSC manufacturing. We identify persistent gaps in the literature — particularly regarding cross-line generalization, 3D organoid analysis, and regulatory-grade validation — and outline directions for future research.
